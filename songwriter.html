About the Writer

Benny Thomas brings 15 years of live performance, 900+ events, and a lifetime of stories to every song he writes. From Omaha to Los Angeles to Las Vegas to Nashville — his journey through America's entertainment capitals gives his songwriting an authentic, hard-earned voice.

His catalog spans heartfelt country ballads, high-energy anthems, and genre-bending originals that blend traditional country storytelling with modern production. Whether it's a summer anthem ready for radio or a tender love song built for a first dance, Benny writes songs that stick.

Endorsed by Spector Bass, Palmer Guitars, Marshall, and Eden Amplifiers. Frontman of Friends with Bennys, Nashville's most unpredictable live band.

"He writes what he lives — and you can hear every mile of the road in every line."
— Dale Jacobs, Promotion Manager
