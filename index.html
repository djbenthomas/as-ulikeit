About

Not just a DJ.
An experience director.

My name is Ben Thomas. For 15 years, I've been helping couples create wedding experiences that people still talk about years later.

I started in Omaha with one of the largest DJ companies in North America, then moved to California to deepen my craft. After working with top LA companies, I became the Manager & Booking Agent at The Rose Garden Estate, where I coordinated over 300 weddings from first inquiry to last dance.

Now based in Nashville, I bring that full-spectrum event experience to every couple. I don't just play music — I manage the timeline, coordinate with your vendors, and make sure every guest walks away saying "that was the best wedding I've been to."
