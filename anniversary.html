Their story

Tom proposed on the sofa in her parents' living room — just watching TV. Her mom was in the kitchen, her dad was already in bed. When she said yes, her dad came stumbling out asking what all the noise was about.

What brought them together? Cars and motorcycles. Tom was a mechanic. Michele loved riding on the back of his bike — until she bought her own dirt bike and street bike so she could ride alongside him.

They started in a basement apartment at his parents' house. Not perfect, but cozy. Baby Andrea arrived, then Ben nine months later. They bought their first house on 41st & Drexel with Tom working as an electrician. Years of hard work, family holidays, Sunday dinners, and playing guitars together in a band for 10 years.

About 40 years later, the kids were grown and they bought their home on 112th St. Every day they count their blessings. They cook together, garden together, and still finish each other's sentences.

"We get along good. We do things together.
We have the same likes and dislikes."
— Michele
