About the Band

Live music that moves people.

Liv N the Dream is a Nashville-based live band made up of top-notch Nashville musicians. We play the fun, danceable music that keeps every generation on the dance floor — from country classics to rock, pop, and party anthems.

From the first dance to the last call, Liv N the Dream tailors the setlist to your crowd. Whether it's a romantic acoustic moment or an all-out party anthem, the band reads the room and delivers.
