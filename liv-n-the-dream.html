About the Band

Live music that moves people.

Liv N the Dream is a Nashville-based live band bringing high-energy performances to weddings and events across Middle Tennessee. Led by Ben Thomas, the band delivers a dynamic mix of country, rock, pop, and Motown — keeping every generation on the dance floor.

From the first dance to the last call, Liv N the Dream tailors the setlist to your crowd. Whether it's a romantic acoustic moment or an all-out party anthem, the band reads the room and delivers.
