About

From Nebraska to Nashville

Benny Thomas is a Nashville-based singer, songwriter, and entertainer whose journey spans 15 years and 900+ events across the country. From Omaha to Los Angeles, Las Vegas, and now Nashville — every mile shows up in the music.

In Las Vegas, he hosted a hit game show and earned a reputation as the world's fastest bass slapper. Today, he channels that same energy into blistering guitar work, freestyle rap, and a live show that's famously unpredictable.

Benny invented "train beat metal" — a signature style that takes popular songs and "countrifies" them, transforming chart-toppers into gritty, high-energy country anthems. His live performances are interactive, often improvising lyrics on the fly and bringing audience members into the moment.

Whether it's a wedding, corporate event, private party, or club show — the Benny Thomas Band delivers a night nobody forgets.
